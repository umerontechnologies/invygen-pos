# Invygen POS — Inventory & Billing App

Invygen POS is an offline-first Flutter application for inventory management, sales, billing, and customer tracking.

Built with Flutter and SQLite, it is designed for small businesses, retail shops, and local sellers who need a fast and reliable local POS system without depending on internet access.

Developed by UMERON Technologies.

## Features

- First-run onboarding and business setup
- Local SQLite database
- Dashboard summary and quick stats
- Products management with stock and low-stock tracking
- Customers management with balance tracking
- Suppliers management
- Sales entry with automatic stock deduction
- Orders list with Pending, Delivered, and Cancelled statuses
- Mark order delivered with stock deduction
- Settings screen

## Tech Stack

- Flutter
- Dart
- SQLite
- Android
